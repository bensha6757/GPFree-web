OUR WORK

Research Areas

LLM Factuality & Safety

Detecting and mitigating hallucinations in large language models. Our work on cross-examination, uncertainty modeling, and the [IDK] token has been published at EMNLP, NeurIPS, and TACL.

Adversarial Robustness & Vision

Defending machine learning models against semantic adversarial attacks. Our StyleAT framework protects face recognition systems from style-based perturbations while preserving model accuracy.

Biomedical & Multimodal AI

Building foundation models that bridge molecular, protein, and textual modalities. Our MAMMAL and BioVERSE architectures enable multi-modal reasoning for drug discovery and biomedical research.
